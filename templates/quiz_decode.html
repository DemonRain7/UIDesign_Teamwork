Decoded — 鱼香肉丝 (Fish-Fragrant Shredded Pork)

You now have the vocabulary. Let's break this dish name down completely.

Flavor Word
鱼香
yú-xiāng

NOT fish — a sweet-sour-savory-spicy sauce made with garlic, ginger, and pickled chilies. No fish is used or needed.

Cooking Method
炒
chǎo / stir-fried

Quick wok toss over high heat — the ingredients stay crisp and the sauce glazes the pork perfectly in seconds.

Main Ingredient
肉丝
ròu-sī / shredded pork

Thin pork strips, often with wood ear mushrooms and bamboo shoots for varied texture.

Overall Vibe

Complex — sweet + sour + savory + mild spice. A glossy reddish-brown sauce clings to tender shredded pork. No fish anywhere.
