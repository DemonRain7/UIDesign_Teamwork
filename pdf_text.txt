LESSON 2
Flavor & Style Words = Taste Preview
These words in a dish name are a preview of what your taste buds will experience.
REGIONAL STYLE
Hunan-style (湘)
Intensely spicy, smoky, garlicky. Direct chili heat — no numbing. Dry and 
fragrant.
REGIONAL STYLE
Cantonese-style (粤)
Light, fresh, ingredient-forward. Minimal seasoning; clean and elegant.
FLAVOR SIGNAL
Mala / Numbing-Spicy (麻辣)
Ma (麻) = numbing from peppercorn + La (辣) = chili burn. Layered and electric.
FLAVOR SIGNAL
Yu-xiang / "Fish-Fragrant" (鱼香)
Contains NO fish. Sweet-sour-savory-spicy sauce (garlic, pickled chili, vinegar).
Hunan ≠ Mala
Hunan (湘) = pure chili heat — hits you straight, dry and smoky.
Mala (麻辣) = numbing + burning — two layered sensations.
